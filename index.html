History

Since our founding in 2021, we recognized the need for a technology to streamline the staffing process. So, we created the industry’s most user-friendly web-based VMS technology, we call iWorkVMS, specifically for our clients and our agencies. Our 360-degree solution supports the healthcare staffing process with ease. It has been strategically designed to be seamless to implement and easy to use, all while intuitively simplifying the hiring process.

Clients

Pre-populated and customized job selections puts HR reps or nurse managers just one-click away from getting their staffing needs out. Configuration of the system creates a streamlined process to approve jobs internally before they reach our network of staffing agencies and the submissions come pouring in. Working with iWorkVMS means no more time spent reviewing candidates who aren’t qualified. iWorkVMS’s relationship teams make sure that the only candidates in the system are qualified for your positions Candidates can be reviewed and hired with the click of a button. Traveler compliance documents are stored during and after each assignment to ensure all needed credentials are accurate and up-to-date. Incredible visibility and reporting in key staffing categories and spend segments can be delivered right from our system.

Why Choose Us

Why You will Give Us Priority

One Contract

All agencies within iWorkVMS’s network are enjoined to a single, standard contract. This eliminates the requirement for our healthcare clients to maintain multiple agency contracts, each with its own terms, conditions, and stipulations.

Single Point of Contact

iWorkVMS offers a single point of contact through a specifically-assigned Relationship Manager supported by a client relationship team that coordinates all communication, information, and client needs throughout the entire staffing process.

Reduced Costs

Our recruiting process includes a large contracted agency pool competing to fill open positions. This enables us to maintain pricing at competitive levels while also improving quality and turn-around time. Additionally, iWorkVMS’s standard agency contract includes terms that substantially lower costs pertaining to orientation, overtime, assignment extensions, and the transitioning of candidates to permanent employment status.

Client Benefits

At iWorkVMS, we pride ourselves on building strategic staffing solutions that provide cost savings, create efficiencies by streamlining communication, contracting, credentialing, and invoicing while at the same staying laser focused on quality and enhancing patient outcomes. By offering a solution that strikes the right balance of high tech and high touch, we give time and money back to our clients so they can focus on delivering the best patient care possible.
