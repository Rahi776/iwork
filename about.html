FEATURES

Why do we stand out so much?

Vendor MSP

As a vendor-neutral Managed Service Provider (MSP) utilizing our state-of-the-art, proprietary VMS technology, iWorkVMS, we significantly reduce temporary staffing expenditures, improve visibility and control, and reduce the risks associated with temporary staffing.

Efficient Staffing

We make the staffing process painless and efficient for our clients and approved agencies participating in our programs through iWorkVMS’s single contract.

Eliminate Burdens

We eliminate burdensome administrative work by assigning each client a dedicated, single point of contact Relationship Team that is personally involved in delivering quality staff.

Accurate Database

Customized reporting tracks spending and key performance metrics, while iWorkVMS, our web-based VMS technology, stores, manages, and monitors supplemental staff credential records to provide essential compliance support.

Physician Locum Ten

As a pioneer in the industry, iWorkVMS was the first to provide physician locum tenens and advanced practice workforce solutions in addition to nursing, allied health, medical office, IT and non-clinical services.

Enterprise-Wide

Today, iWorkVMS is the only MSP with VMS technology specifically built and dedicated to provide an enterprise-wide, comprehensive MSP solution for any healthcare organization looking to simplify its supplemental staffing process.
